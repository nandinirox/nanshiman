Exploratory Travel

Travel guides and books can only take you so far. But this web application will help you navigate the unwritten rules, important places to travel, the tips and tricks, personal experience and so much more with just the click of a button.

Joginder Valley

Bir-Billing is a village settled in the Joginder Valley of Himachal Pradesh. The place portrays a typical and idyllic village setting. However, very close to the village you will find an entirely different picture. The entire sky is filled with paragliders enjoying this adventure sport. The hills and the climate make Bir-Billing the perfect spot to go paragliding. It is for this reason Bir-Billing is called the paragliding capital of the nation.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh is located on the banks of the Ganga River in the northern state of Uttarakhand. The city is mostly a pilgrimage center and is also known as the yoga capital of India. There are, however, certain adventure activities conducted here for thrill-seeking tourists. You can engage in activities such as rafting in the Ganga River, rock and cliff climbing, take part in a survival camp at the nearby forest or go bungee jumping. The bungee jump is the highest in India at a staggering 83 meters high – leaving you in freefall for a whole 5 seconds.

Dandeli

Dandeli is located on the Karnataka – Goa border in Karnataka. The place attracts a lot of tourists from Goa since it is only 125 kilometers from the state. Dandeli offers a plethora of activities like trekking, wildlife spotting, and photography, kayaking, and fishing. There is also a sanctuary located quite close to Dandeli where you can take a jeep safari and spot birds and wildlife.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is not all about royal palaces and forts. It is a land that will also delight the adventure lover in you. Lately, hot air ballooning is gaining traction in the state. You can either choose to train in the science of hot air ballooning and then perform the activity under guided supervision or you can take a hot air balloon tour. The tour throws an entirely new perspective on Rajasthan and will give you a bird’s eye view of the cities of Jaisalmer, Jodhpur and Jaipur.
